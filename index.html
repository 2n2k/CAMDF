Welcome to the CAMDF CTF!

This CTF was made as a project by the Vilnius University Information Systems and Cybersecurity students Adris and Matas.

The challenges were made for beginners so all of them should be pretty easy to complete.

Our goal:

Our goal during the creation of CAMDF CTF was to give a possibility for beginners to try out their skill in different Cybersecurity challenges of varying difficulty.
Also, the CTF was a ground for both of us to test our knowledge and capabilities of creating and managing a CTF platform.


We hope You like it, and we wish You the best of luck! - Adris and Matas
